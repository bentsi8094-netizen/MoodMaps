# 🌍 MoodMaps: The Emotional Topography of Connection

Welcome to **MoodMaps**, a futuristic social ecosystem designed to transform how we perceive and share human emotion in real-time. 

In a world of static profiles, MoodMaps offers a **Living Canvas** of the collective psyche, where every state of mind is a coordinate and every connection is meaningful.

## 🌌 The Vision
MoodMaps isn't just an app; it's a window into the current emotional state of the world around you. By combining **Generative AI** with **Dynamic Geolocation**, we've created a space where your mood is visualized through curated aesthetics and temporary, high-impact interactions. 

We believe that human emotions are ephemeral, vibrant, and ever-changing—and our technology reflects that through our unique **24-Hour Sliding Session** architecture.

## 🛠 Features that Define the Future
- **🧠 AI-Empathy Core**: Our integrated AI Agent doesn't just process text; it mirrors your emotions, matches your wit, and curates visual stickers that represent your unique state of mind.
- **🛰 Real-time Emotional Mapping**: See where the world is happy, contemplative, or energized. Our high-precision mapping engine visualizes the "Mood Markers" of everyone around you.
- **♻ Absolute Sliding Lifecycle**: A revolutionary session model where your data lives and breathes with you. A session stays alive as long as you are active, naturally fading after 24 hours of silence to ensure a fresh, relevant feed every single day.
- **💎 Premium Aesthetics**: Designed with a high-end, futuristic dark-mode palette, featuring glassmorphism, smooth animations, and a seamless sliding navigation system.

## 🏗 Tech Stack
- **Frontend**: React Native & Expo (The pinnacle of cross-platform performance).
- **State Engine**: Zustand (Ultra-fast, persisted global state).
- **Backend Hub**: Node.js & Express.
- **Digital Vault**: Supabase (PostgreSQL with real-time capabilities).
- **Intelligence**: OpenAI (GPT-4o-mini).

## 🚀 The Future
MoodMaps is paving the way for **Emotional Intelligence as a Service (EIaaS)**. We are moving towards a world where digital interaction feels as natural and fluid as a real-life conversation, where privacy is absolute, and where every "Mood" contributes to a beautiful, global tapestry of human experience.
